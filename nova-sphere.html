Tap. Talk. Listen.

Tap the sphere and speak. The built-in INMP441 microphone captures your voice, processes it locally, sends it to your AI, and speaks the response back through the speaker. The face reacts in real time.

# Full voice pipeline — runs locally

Tap → INMP441 mic (16kHz I2S)

  → faster-whisper (STT, offline)

  → Ollama/gemma3 (LLM, local)

  → edge-tts (48kHz neural voice)

  → Speaker (full duplex)

✓ No audio leaves your network
✓ Replaceable: swap any component (use Piper for fully offline TTS)
✓ WebSocket protocol for real-time bidirectional audio
